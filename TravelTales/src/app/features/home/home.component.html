WELCOME TO TRAVEL TALES - YOUR JOURNEY

Every trip you take tells a story. At Travel Tales, we help you capture those stories and turn your travels into a visual journey you'll love revisiting.

Whether you're a world explorer, a weekend wanderer, or just getting started, Travel Tales is your personal travel companion - designed to make remembering your adventures as exciting as living them.

Pin Your Travels on the Map

See the world like never before! As you log each country you've visited, it comes alive on your personal world map — beautifully colored to show where your footprints have landed.

Keep Track of Every City

From cozy towns to buzzing capitals, add every city you've explored. View them all neatly organized in a table — your travels, in perfect order.

Relive Your Moments with Travel Cards

Each destination gets its own Travel Card — featuring a photo you upload, the name of the city and country, and the date you visited. It’s like a mini postcard from your own journey.
